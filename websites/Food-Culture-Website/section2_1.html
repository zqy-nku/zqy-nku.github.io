Map for U.S. Restaurants

Here is a general map showing the distribution of restaurants around the United State. Each city is corresponds to a circle, where its color represents the states it lies in and the its size shows the number of restaurants it owns. From the distribution results, we could derive that Nevada and Arizona has more restaurants than other cities in America.
